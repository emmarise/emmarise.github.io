A personal logistics assistant that sends a beautifully formatted daily email every morning. Tracks upcoming trips with progressive detail reveal (flight info, packing lists, and itineraries appear as departure approaches), recurring weekly events with "today" and "tomorrow" alerts, and maintenance tasks with color-coded overdue badges. Orchestrated by n8n running in Docker, backed by Supabase Postgres, and delivered via Resend.
