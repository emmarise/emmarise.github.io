A chat-based personal finance app where you talk to track expenses. Powered by Groq (Llama 3.3 70B) for natural language parsing — supports English and Chinese input, multi-day bulk entries, "Ask My Data" analytics, and recurring expense automation. Built with Next.js 16, Supabase for auth and storage, shadcn/ui (base-nova) for the UI, and deployed on Vercel.
